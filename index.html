Introduction

Welcome to the Texas State Roleplay Staff Guide.

This guide explains expectations for staff and how things run day-to-day.

Staff are responsible for keeping the server fair, organised, and enjoyable.

Your actions represent the server, so professionalism is required at all times.

If you need help, open a Board of Directives ticket in communications.

This guide may be updated at any time.

WARNING

Do not leak or share any staff-only information.

Violations may result in removal or staff blacklist.

Infractions System

2 Strikes = Demotion/Suspension

3 Strikes = Termination

Warning Types

Warning - Logged issue

Strike - Serious offence

Demotion - Rank removal

Suspension - Temporary removal

Termination - Permanent removal

Blacklist - Permanent staff ban
