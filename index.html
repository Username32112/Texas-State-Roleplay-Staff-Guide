Texas State Roleplay Staff Guide

Introduction

Welcome to the Texas State Roleplay Staff Guide.

This guide is here to help you understand what you need to know as a staff member and how things are expected to run day to day. It’s not meant to overcomplicate anything — just a simple reference you can come back to whenever you’re unsure.

Being staff means you’re helping keep the server fair, organised, and enjoyable for everyone. The way you act in-game and in the community reflects directly on the server, so staying professional and consistent is important.

If you ever have questions, open a Board of Directives ticket in the communication server and leadership will assist you as soon as possible.

This guide can be updated at any time. It’s your responsibility to stay updated.

WARNING

Do not leak, share, or distribute any part of this staff guide or internal staff information.

Anyone caught leaking will face immediate removal from staff and may be staff blacklisted depending on severity.

Information

Higher Rank Decisions

All decisions made by High Rank staff are final and must be respected. If you disagree, submit an Internal Affairs ticket instead of arguing in-game or publicly.

Commitment

Staff are expected to complete 4 hours weekly.
Promotion track staff must complete 8 hours weekly.
Activity is required to remain and progress in staff.

Discord Nickname

Format: [RANK] ┃ [Username]
Example: TM┃Yuyu565660

Custom Messages

Only Lead Moderators+ can use custom messages.
Lower ranks require permission before using any custom tools.

Shift Rules

Minimum shift: 15 minutes.
Must have 15+ players online unless told otherwise.
Must remain active (no idling unless on break).

Notifications

Always warn users before kicking or banning them.

Infractions & Discipline

Escalation System

3 Warnings = 1 Strike
2 Strikes = Demotion/Suspension
3 Strikes = Termination

Warning Types

Notice – Small correction, no record impact.

Warning – Logged mistake or repeated issue.

Strike – Serious behavioural issue.

Demotion – Rank removal or downgrade.

Suspension – Temporary removal from staff.

Termination – Permanent removal from staff.

Staff Blacklist – Permanent ban from all staff positions.
