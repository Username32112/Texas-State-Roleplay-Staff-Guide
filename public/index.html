Welcome

Welcome to the Texas State Roleplay Staff Guide.

This guide explains staff expectations, responsibilities, procedures, and rules.

Staff Information

Higher Rank Decisions

All decisions made by the High Ranking Team are final and must be respected.

If you believe a decision is incorrect or unfair, submit an Internal Affairs ticket for review.

Allowed Not Allowed
Create an Internal Affairs ticket Argue publicly
Explain concerns professionally Disrespect High Rank decisions
Wait for a proper review Create staff drama

Requirements

Active Engagement

During your shift, you are expected to actively monitor and moderate the server.

Standing still or doing nothing during your shift is not allowed unless you have properly selected On Break in Melonly.

Activity Expectation
Discord Checks Make sure everyone in the server is in the discord server.
Livery Checks Ensure all departments have the right livery.
Vehicle Checks Make sure everyone is driving vehicles that they are allowed to drive.
Mod Calls Respond to situations when needed.

Shift Requirements

Requirement Rule
Minimum Shift Length 15 Minutes
Minimum Players Online 15 Players
Breaks Must use Melonly On Break

Shift time must always be earned through active moderation and presence.

Kick Procedure

Before kicking a player, staff must:

  • Inform the player they are being kicked.
  • Explain the reason clearly.
  • Remain professional.
  • Ensure they understand the cooldown.

Approved Kick Message 


:pm [User] You are being kicked for 30 minutes for [Reason]. Do not rejoin during this time or you will be banned.

Ban Procedure

Before banning a player, staff must:

  • Explain the reason for the ban.
  • Provide appeal information.
  • Log the punishment correctly.

Approved Ban Message 


:pm [User] You are being banned from Texas State Roleplay for [Reason]. If you wish to appeal this punishment, please submit an appeal through our communication server.

Appeal Code: TEXASR

Commands & Permissions

Command Usage

Staff members must use commands responsibly and only for moderation purposes.

Commands must never be used for fun, personal benefit, or to interfere with roleplay.

Allowed Not Allowed
Using commands during moderation situations Using commands for entertainment
Helping players when necessary Using commands to benefit yourself
Following rank permissions Using restricted commands without permission

Command Formatting

Staff should not use short commands.

You have a higher chance of teleporting to someone else and you will also be infracted for it.
Example Reason
:to yuyu So you don't teleport to someone else.
:to yuy You

Discipline System

Staff Infractions & Discipline Policy

Staff infractions are used to maintain accountability, professionalism, and proper staff standards.

They help track behavior, correct mistakes, and ensure staff members follow expectations.

Escalation System

Infraction Level Result
3 Warnings 1 Strike
2 Strikes Demotion or Suspension
3 Strikes Termination
Trial Moderator 3 Warnings / 1 Strike may result in termination

Notice

A Notice is a reminder given for minor mistakes that require improvement.

Examples Result
Small moderation mistakes Reminder to improve
Forgetting staff duties Guidance
Low shift activity Staff feedback

Warning

A Warning is the first official punishment and is logged on a staff record.

Examples Result
Repeated mistakes Logged Warning
Unprofessional behavior Logged Warning
Minor misuse of staff tools Logged Warning

Strike

A Strike is a serious punishment requiring immediate improvement.

Examples Result
Command abuse Strike Added
Ignoring responsibilities Strike Added
Disrespecting others Strike Added

Demotion

A Demotion removes a staff member from their current rank due to ongoing issues or lack of improvement.

Possible Result
Moved down a rank
May need to re-earn rank
Possible probation period

Suspension

A Suspension temporarily removes staff duties and permissions.

Examples
Serious misconduct
Staff abuse
Toxic behavior
Ongoing investigation

Termination

Termination permanently removes a staff member from the staff team.

Examples
Repeated major offenses
Major staff abuse
Harming the community
Ignoring previous punishments

Staff Blacklist

The Staff Blacklist is the highest punishment and prevents someone from returning to staff.

Examples
Severe abuse of power
Leaking staff information
Major trust violations
Serious harassment
